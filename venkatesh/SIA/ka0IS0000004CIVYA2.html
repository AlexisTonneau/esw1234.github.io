Q. Are KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards applicable to all KrisFlyer members?
A. KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards are applicable to all KrisFlyer and KrisFlyer Elite members. KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards are awarded automatically when you have earned the required Elite miles on flights operated by Singapore Airlines, or Scoot in a single calendar year, from 01 January to 31 December.
 

Q. Can PPS Club members earn KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards as well?
A. No, KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards are applicable to KrisFlyer and KrisFlyer Elite members only. PPS Club members can earn exclusive PPS Rewards, based on their accrued PPS Value in their current membership year.
 

Q. How can I track the KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards I have?
A. To track your KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards, log into your KrisFlyer account on singaporeair.com or via your SingaporeAir mobile app, then go to the “KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards” tab or “Your rewards” tab under your Account Summary. Your KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards progress tracker and applicable rewards that you have earned will be shown here.
 

Q. How do I use my KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards?
A. To access your KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards, log into your KrisFlyer account on singaporeair.com or via your SingaporeAir mobile app, then go to the “KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards” tab or “Your rewards“ tab under your Account Summary.

Download the Kris+ app to redeem the 1,500 KrisPay miles awarded, and visit KrisShop.com to redeem the S$20 KrisShop Promo code.

For all other KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards, you can use the reward(s) on your eligible booking(s) online. If you require further assistance, contact KrisFlyer Membership Services.
 

Q. Are the KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards transferable?
A.KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards cannot be transferred. However, you can use the Short-haul Advance Upgrade and Premium Economy Advance Upgrade rewards for bookings for your redemption nominees.

 

Q. I have misplaced the promo code for KrisShop. Can I request for a new promo code

A. You can retrieve the promo code from “Messages”. Simply log into your KrisFlyer account on singaporeair.com or via your SingaporeAir mobile app and click on the “Messages” tab to retrieve the relevant reward message.

KrisShop promo codes that have expired cannot be re-issued.

Extension of Expiring KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards

Q. Are my KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards eligible for extension?
A. If you have the following Rewards that are expiring in 2022 or 2023, they are eligible for extension:
  1. 1,500 KrisPay miles
  2. 25% Bonus KrisFlyer Miles
  3. 50% Bonus KrisFlyer Miles
  4. 5,000 KrisFlyer Miles Redemption Discount
  5. Double KrisFlyer Miles
  6. Short-haul Advance Upgrade
  7. Premium Economy Advance Upgrade

These Rewards will now expire on 31 December 2023. This extension will be processed automatically, and no action is required on your part.

Q. How do I check if I have expiring KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards?
A. Log in to your KrisFlyer account on singaporeair.com or the SingaporeAir mobile app and navigate to Account Summary > KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards. Your Rewards and their respective expiry dates will be listed under "Manage rewards".

Q. I logged into my account and noticed that the KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards eligible for extension have not been extended. How can I check that my Rewards have been properly extended?
A. The new expiry date of eligible KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards will be reflected in your account by 31 December 2022. Please log in here to view if you have KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards.

Q. Can I further extend the validity of the extended KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards for use beyond 31 December 2023?
A. KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards may be used on flights departing after 31 December 2023, if the Rewards are applied on or before 31 December 2023. Otherwise, please utilise your KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards by 31 December 2023.

Q. I had applied a KrisFlyer Milestone Reward on my booking that has now been refunded. Will the Reward be redeposited to my account?
A. For the following Rewards:
    1. 25% Bonus KrisFlyer Miles
    2. 50% Bonus KrisFlyer Miles
    3. Double KrisFlyer Miles
Please log in to your KrisFlyer account and navigate Account Summary > KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards to manage your Rewards. You may click on “Remove” to remove your Reward application from the booking. This will change the status back to “Available”, and its expiry date will be updated to 31 December 2023 in 4-6 weeks.

For the following Rewards:
    1. 5,000 KrisFlyer Miles Redemption Discount
    2. Short-haul Advance Upgrade
    3. Premium Economy Advance Upgrade
Please contact KrisFlyer Membership Services to redeposit your Reward. Their expiry date will be updated to 31 December 2023.
