Pointers to ensure member receive miles

  • Booking name must match passport and KrisFlyer membership record. 
  • Member to quote their KrisFlyer membership number at time of flight and auxiliary bookings. 
  • Member to present their KrisFlyer membership card at the airport check-in counter. 

 

 

Mileage claim may be rejected due to the following scenarios

  • Family Name/Given Name mismatch **(email notification will be sent to member for failed accrual)
  • Invalid travel class **(email notification will be sent to member for failed accrual)
  • Member is not eligible for FFP, Elite and PPS miles
  • Redemption ticket not eligible for credit
  • Discounted ticket is not eligible for credit (e.g., Group/Staff travel)
  • Member is a no-show or offloaded
  • Flight exclusion check rule failed for Accrual – ticketing term
  • Customer type not eligible for Accrual (e.g., when customer type of KF account is tagged as Board Member)
  • Cash paid for FFK redemption ticket is zero

FAQs

Q. I travelled on SQ flights last month and just enrolled as a KrisFlyer member. Can I accrue miles on those flights that I have taken?
A. Yes. A newly-enrolled KrisFlyer member can submit a retroactive mileage claim for SQ flights travelled up to 6 months prior to enrolment. Members claiming for flights travelled on air partners can submit the claim but subject to air partner’s approval.

 

Q. My retroactive mileage claim is rejected due to name mismatch. Why is that so?
A. The name on your ticket must be the same as your KrisFlyer account. 
Discrepancy of the member’s name as booked on the ticket and registered on your KrisFlyer account may cause the request to be rejected. 
(If member insists on escalating, staff should say that we will liaise with the relevant department.)
