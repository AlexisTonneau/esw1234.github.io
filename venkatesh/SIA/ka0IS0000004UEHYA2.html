Inflight Meals (Route Menu)

1. Inflight meals refer to the standard non-SPML/BTC meals offered during the flight.

2. Selection of infight meals are subject to availability on board during meal service. Pre-order of inflight meals is available in Suites/FCL/JCL/SCL.

3. Modifications to Authentically Asian inflight meals (Shi Quan Shi Mei / Shi Quan Wei Mei, Kyo Kaiseki/ Hanakoireki, Shahi Thali/ Ruchi Thali, Hansik) are to be strictly rejected. Passenger who insists on modifications to Authentically Asian options due to preferences or medical/ religious reasons should be advised to select an appropriate standard SPML instead.


4. Additional/specific request of ingredients (e.g. additional portion of rice/gravy) can be acceded to, based on the following empowerment matrix.



Pre-select Inflight Meal
With a confirmed booking in Suites, First, Business or Premium Economy Class, passengers may pre-select their main course for all meal services.
  • Pre-ordering is available 15 days before flight departure, simply log on to Manage Booking with their booking reference to select their meal.

Refer to SAA for more information on dining on board.


View Inflight Menu Online
Passengers can preview their in-flight food, beverage, amenity and snack offerings before their flight via the digital in-flight menu up to eight days before their flight. The menu will also highlight any existing regulatory restrictions that may impact the availability of their meals on board.    ​
 
Alternatively, passengers may also view their menu on board by connecting to our on-board Wi-Fi* to scan the QR code found in the KrisWorld entertainment system.
  • Access to the inflight menu onboard is complimentary and will not deplete their inflight Wi-Fi plan​

*Wi-Fi is available on board on all aircraft except the Boeing 737-800NG.

Refer to SAA for more information on dining on board.
