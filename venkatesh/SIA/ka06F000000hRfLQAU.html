Enhancements to the Redemption Waitlist process

a) Managing of waitlist redemption bookings online

Members will be able to manage their waitlist redemption bookings online on singaporeair.com. 

  • Members will be able to select available alternative flights for immediate redemption and proceed to issue their redemption booking online. 
  • Simply log in to Singaporeair.com, retrieve the waitlisted booking and click “Select Alternative Flights”.

 

b) Receive reminders on waitlist redemption bookings

Periodic reminders will be send to keep members updated about their waitlisted redemption flights. 

  • Members with waitlisted redemption bookings will receive these reminders at pre-determined intervals to the flight departure date, at
    • -9mths,
    • -6mths,
    • -3mths,
    • -1mth and
    • -3 weeks to departure.


Members will be able to opt out of such reminders on their waitlisted redemption bookings via their KrisFlyer account online, under Profile > Preferences.

 

c) Changes to confirmation and cancellation of waitlist redemption bookings

  • Waitlist for redemption flights will be available up to 3 weeks before departure, and members will be informed of the outcome of their waitlist redemptions at least 2 weeks before flight departure. 
  • All unsuccessful redemption waitlists will be cancelled 2 weeks prior to the flights. 
    • Cancellation will apply to all waitlist redemptions, excluding redemption upgrades, which will be available up to the point of departure.
  • Members will be informed via email and SMS when their pending redemption waitlists are cancelled.
  • Members with pending redemption waitlists will duly receive reminder notifications at the appropriate junctures, before any cancellation.
