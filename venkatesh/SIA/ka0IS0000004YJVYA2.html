  1. Process Flowchart
    Name Correction INF and ADT

 

Suggested Script

(a) Mr/Mrs Xxx, we are unable to amend the name on your booking as other airlines sectors are involved. Alternatively, we will assist you to make a new booking and issue a ticket with the correct name. Once a new ticket is issued, we will accord full refund of your present ticket.

 

(b) Mr/Mrs Xxx, may I have your name as it appears on your passport?

 

(c) Mr/Mrs Xxx, there will be a service fee of USD 50.00 to amend the name and reissue the ticket.

 

(d) Mr/Mrs Xxx, we normally collect a service fee of USD 50.00 for name correction. However, we will waive the service fee for this transaction and process the changes. 

 

(e) Mr/Mrs Xxx, may I have the infant’s name as it appears on the passport?

 

 

2. Name Correction SOP

      • Penalty Fee: USD50
      • Staff to request customer to provide name as per passport found under the MRZ (Machine-Readable Zone). 
        • If customer have difficulty to provide correct name sequence, even with our guidance, staff can request for a passport copy. 

e.g. "LAST<NAME<<FIRST<NAME<ADDITIONAL<NAMES<<<<<".   

      • Addition of names (limited to 1 phrase only), with no change in the rest of the name.
      • Deletion of names (limited to 1 phrase only), with no change in the rest of the name.
         

Below Name Correction is allowed with fee waived. 

      • Change of salutation
      • Double family name (e.g, TAN/TANMR)
      • Change in name sequence, with no change in spelling (e.g., AHKOW/TANMR)
      • Full name in family name field instead of family name only (e.g, TanAhKow/Mr / mainly for SAA bookings)
      • Missing middle name due to SAA design
      • Genuine error (e.g., typo error/wrong family name/repeated names)
      • Change of name legally
        • Supporting document such as deed poll/marriage cert is required for verification.

 

3a. ARD Guide for Name Correction

Step by Step Guide for Name Correction

Step 1: Click on Re-Price Reissue E-TKT Manually

Step 2: Only DELETE TST for passenger with incorrect name

 

Step 3: Click on New TST and click on next step.

 

Step 4: Select the passenger with incorrect name

 

Step 5: Click on update fares

 

Step 6: Scroll down, check the fare and click on next step

 

Step 7: Again, click on next step

 

Step 8: Click on Add Original Document (per pax) and then End of Transaction.

Example: If there are two passengers and need to click on Add Original Document twice

 

Step 9: Delete the E-ticket of the passenger that needs to be changed

 

Step 10: Click Change on PNR > Update the correct name and click on OK then do EOT

 

Step 11: Click on Issue Document for the issuance of the TKT

 

3b. ARD Guide for Ticket Association

Step by Step Guide for Ticket Association

Step 1: Go to "Add Document Number"

 

Step 2:

    • Select which passenger needs the ticket to be associated
    • Select which flights the ticket to be associated
    • Click all applicable flights as per the ticket coupon

 

Step 3:

    • Add the E-Ticket Number
    • Enter the coupon number. It should match the ticket and the itinerary

 

Step 4:

    • Click 'Add E-Ticket Number'
    • Click 'Close'

 

The E-ticket is now associated.

 

 

 

3c. ARD Guide to Add INF-SSR

Step by Step Guide to Add INF details through SSRs

Step 1: Go to 'Add Service'

 

Step 2:

    • Select ‘Passenger Information’
    • Select ‘Infant’
    • Select which passenger to associate the INF details
    • Fill in the details
    • Click ‘Book services’

 

INF details are now added on SSRs

 


4. Name Correction Smart Flow

Steps to use name correction smart flow (Commercial)

Important Note

  • Name Correction Smart Flow cannot be used on CHD and INF ticket
  • Prior to running the smart flow agent needs to copy ticket number, add the original document and delete the ticket.

 

Step 1: Go to add original document

 

Step 2:  Delete the ticket from the PNR

 

Step 3: Go on the Smart Flow and add ticket number

 

Step 4: Add the Passenger number/ Family name/ Given name with salutation and sector number.

 

Step 5 – You can add the service fee on the same smart flow.

 

Step 6: Enter the PNR on the Smart Flow

 

Step 7: Enter the staff name and the passenger number

 

Step 8: Enter the line number with staff name and add the ticket number without hypen.

 

Step 9: Add the ‘Arrange FOP’ and ‘Add TSM FOP’ for the reissue and issue the documents.

Outcome – The tickets will be reissued with the corrected name and the TSM will be collected.

6. Name Correction for SQ ticket with OAL flight
 

This refers to OAL flights including codeshare on SQ tickets (618 document).

Passenger may have made a genuine error on the name when making a booking (Ticketed/Not Ticketed).

As there may be system constraints if any for names to be correctly reflected in the OAL system with ticket reissuance, name correction or name change on booking or tickets with OAL sectors is strictly not allowed.

A new ticket needs to be issued instead for name correction or name change.

SOP
1. Make a new booking with the same RBD
2. Issue ticket on the new PNR and process a full refund of the ticket with the incorrect name
