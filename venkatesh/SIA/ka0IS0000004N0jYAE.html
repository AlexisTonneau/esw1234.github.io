AOUT OF SEQUENCE In general, out of sequence is not allowed and tickets are to be reassessed, except for the stated scenarios and applicable to 618 tickets on SQ flights only. Upon approval to travel out-of-sequence,
(i) collect no-show fee (via EMD) if applicable
(ii) change the unused sector from OPEN to EXCHANGED (reissuance not required)
(iii) insert IDLC SK to prevent downline cancellation
(iv) insert CKIN remarks
(v) L2 to update tracker 
Staff must inform passenger there will be no refund of the unused sector(s).
10EXTENSION OF EXPIRING MILES / REINSTATEMENT OF EXPIRED MILESNon-purged expired miles refer to miles which have expired within the previous 2 months, but not yet purged by the system.
Purged expired miles refer to extended expired miles or miles which have expired more than 2 months.

1.  For “Why-Not” scenarios, L1s empowered to approve up to 100k miles per member & DMs to approve cases above 100k miles per member (with fee waived) 
2a.  For “Non-Why-Not” scenarios, accede to extensions /reinstatement requests with fees collected if below conditions are met:
   i.  This is member’s first time requesting for an extension / reinstatement 
  ii.  Date of expiry of miles is not more than 1 year ago.  
2b.  If above conditions do not meet, requests should be declined as a first cut; 
   i.  Appeals for fee waiver requests or for amounts exceeding 100k miles will be escalated to our DMs for approval.
   ii. Appeals for exceptional consideration on cases will be escalated to DMs (up to 250k miles) or CCMs (above 250k miles) for their approval. 
3. All approved requests on miles extensions will be extended for 6 months, from date of request.

If approved, purged miles will be reinstated as service recovery (email CCSD_Backoffice@singaporeair.com.sg for the crediting.
These miles are not extendable and the validity of the reinstated miles will be 6 months from the date of request.
For extension of expiring/non-purged expired miles (up to the privious 2 months), service fee can be waived if they are supported with valid reasons.
(For PPS members who redeemed miles for an award ticket as a non-PPS member and subsequently refunded the ticket as a PPS member with expired miles, service fees to be waived, upon request only. Purged miles will not be reinstated.)
