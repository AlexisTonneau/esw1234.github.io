To carry instrument as a cabin-seat baggage, it should not exceed the following size and weight limitations: 

  • Boeing 737 aircraft*: 44cm (height) X 30cm (width) x 30cm (depth); or
  • Non-Boeing 737 aircraft*: 127cm (height) X 45cm (width) x 30cm (depth); and  
  • 40kg in weight.
*If unsure of the type of aircraft for the flight, refer to the Flight Schedule page on SAA.
