Background
For KrisFlyer Basic, Elite Silver and Elite Gold members
  • KrisFlyer miles will expire after three years (or one year for KrisFlyer miles received from contests) at 23:59 hours Singapore time (GMT +08:00 hours) on the last day of the equivalent month in which they were earned.
e.g. KrisFlyer miles credited to a member’s KrisFlyer account in July 2017 will expire at 23:59 hours Singapore time (GMT +08:00 hours) on 31 July 2020.

PPS Club members
  • KrisFlyer miles will not expire when member remains a PPS Club member.
    •     Applies to all valid KrisFlyer miles, regardless of how they were earned, except for certain promotional KrisFlyer miles, as specified in the specific promotions’ terms and conditions.
  • If member is unable to requalify for PPS Club membership, their KrisFlyer miles will reset to expire after three years at 23:59 hours Singapore time (GMT +08:00 hours) on the last day of the equivalent month at the point which the member fails to requalify and becomes an Elite member.
 
 
KF Miles Expiry
KrisFlyer miles that are expiring for the first time may be extended for a fee. 
KrisFlyer Basic MembersMiles may be extended once for a period of six months
Elite Silver & Elite GoldMay extend their miles for a period of one year only

When a member qualifies into the PPS Club, all valid KrisFlyer miles in their account at point of qualification will no longer expire.
 

Expiring KF miles reflected on SAA
Expiring KF miles will only reflect on SAA when it is within the next 6 months. Otherwise, SAA will display "You don't have KrisFlyer miles expiring in the next 6 months." 

e.g. A member with miles expiring in the next 8 months will see the above message. Whereas a member with miles expiring in the next 5 months will see the breakdown of his miles, expiring within the next 12 months. 
 
 
KF Miles Extension
When a member extends the miles online, service fee will be deducted from the next nearest expiring batch. 
 
 

Any offline assistance rendered for mile extension, member will be charged the miles extension service fee and offline service fees.

KF Miles Extension / Reinstatement SOP
 


Definition
Reinstatement of Miles Reinstatement of miles refers to the process of restoring previously deducted or expired miles back into a member's KrisFlyer account.
Extension of Miles Extension of miles involves prolonging the validity of miles that are nearing their expiration date, allowing the member more time to use them.
Purged expired miles Extended expired miles or miles which have expired more than 2 months. Purged miles will be reinstated as service recovery miles.
Non-purged expired miles Miles which have expired before the next 2 months, but not yet purged by the system.


Note
  • Upon assisting member to extend miles offline, staff to inform the revised expiry date MM/YY. 
  • Once approval is given to reinstate purged expired miles with offline service fee, and the miles is credited to KF account; encourage member to immediately use the mileage. If need be, help to make the booking and issue the tickets.
    • This is to avoid repeated calls and members to re-appeal, claiming they overlook on the expiry miles again. 
  • Refer to SAA for the service fees.


What staff should do
1. What happens if a member has reached out to us more than once to reinstate / extend their miles due to “Why Not” scenarios? 
 
Should members request an extension / reinstatement for a second time under the same WhyNot situation, supporting documents should be provided and sighted first if applicable.  
 
[Frontliners can continue to exercise empowerment under the Why-Not initiative to grant extensions / reinstatement of members’ miles.]
 
2. What happens if a member has reached out to us more than once to reinstate / extend their miles under “Non-Why Not” scenarios? 
 
L1s should reject such requests and encourage members to explore our non-air options on https://www.singaporeair.com/en_UK/sg/ppsclub-krisflyer/use-miles/otherways-to-redeemmiles/.
 
3. If members had previously paid for the extension of their miles on SAA, would this be considered as miles that were extended before? 
 
If members had previously paid for the auto extension of their miles on SAA, this will not be considered as miles that were extended before. If members were not accorded any goodwill approvals before, L1s / DMs may exercise their empowerment to grant a one-time goodwill approval. 
 
4. Can the L1s today escalate to DMs / CCSD Back Office directly without going through the L2s? 
 
The L1s should escalate the cases to their L2s / TLs, who will then escalate these cases to the DMs for their review or CCSD BO for their action. 
 
5. Do staff need to manually override the extension period when extending non-purged miles in MARMS?
 
Yes, new expiry date will be six months from date of approval.
 
6. Under non-Why Not Scenarios, if a ticket/request consist of mileage from both beyond and within a year from request, what will be the approach?
 
Only mileage with expiry within a year from request can be reinstated/extended.
 
7. Are CEM Waivers required for these transactions?  
 
CEM Waivers are required for any waivers of service fees for extending the miles, but no CEM waivers are required for the extended miles itself.

Yes. L1s or DMs will be empowered to reinstate / extend member’s miles accordingly as per the empowerment limits due medical, under “Why Not”.

 Yes. A refund can be done for the couple’s tickets and all their miles may be returned to their Krisflyer account.

Yes. L1s or DMs will be empowered to reinstate / extend member’s miles accordingly as per the empowerment limits due compassionate, under “Why Not”.

FAQ
Q. Do my KrisFlyer miles have expiry date?
A. The miles will expire after 3 years on the last day of the month in which they were earned for Elite Gold   members and below. KrisFlyer miles of PPS members will not expire as long as the member remains a PPS Club member.
 
Q. How many times can my KrisFlyer miles be extended?
A. Miles can only be extended once and for a maximum period of 6 months for Basic and 12 months for Elite members at a service fee.    
 
Q. I have some miles that have expired last week. Can my expired miles be extended?
A. Miles that have already expired cannot be extended.
 
Q. When does the miles expire?
A. Miles will expire at 23:59 hours Singapore time (GMT +08:00 hours) on the last day of the equivalent month in which they were earned.
 
Q. How do I check on the expiry date of my KrisFlyer miles?
A. Log in to your KrisFlyer account on singaporeair.com or the SingaporeAir mobile app and navigate to Miles > Miles Validity. A monthly breakdown of miles expiring in the next 12 months will be shown to you on this page.

Q. When will the extension of my expiring KrisFlyer miles take place?
A. If your miles are eligible for extension, the extension will only take effect at the end of the month they are originally scheduled to expire.

Q. I have miles that have been extended to expire in July 2023. As I would like to use the miles for a flight redemption, must my flight redemption also be completed by July 2023?
A. You will need to redeem your miles before they expire on the last day of the month at 23:59 (GMT +8hrs). Please ensure that the redemption booking is successfully ticketed on or before 31 July 2023. Your travel date may be after July 2023.
