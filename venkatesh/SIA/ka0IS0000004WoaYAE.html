KrisFlyer members can earn KF miles when travelling on Scoot.

  • Mileage accrual for itineraries involving only Scoot-operated flights is based on the amount spent on fares and selected ancillaries.
  • Itineraries involving both Scoot and Singapore Airlines operated flights will be based on actual miles flown and booking class.
  • Mix miles and cash on Flyscoot.com to offset fares, taxes and selected ancillaries for itineraries involving only Scoot-operated flights.
Terms & Conditions apply

For flights purchased on Flyscoot.com, 25% bonus KrisFlyer miles (tier bonus) applies for every Singapore dollar spent on base fare and add-ons, and excludes spend on e-Visa, travel insurance or Scoot protect.
For Scoot flights flown as part of a mixed itinerary, bonus will be 25% of actual mileage flown for the Scoot sector.

 

 

Elite Miles

KrisFlyer members can earn Elite miles on flights operated by Scoot (TR). This will count towards their KrisFlyer Elite membership status renewal/upgrade, as well as KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards.

From 17 January 2022 onwards, KrisFlyer members will earn 2.5 Elite miles (increase from 1 Elite mile) for every KrisFlyer mile earned from flights taken on Scoot, which will then count towards KrisFlyer Elite membership status renewal/upgrade, as well as KrisFlyer Milestone Rewards.
