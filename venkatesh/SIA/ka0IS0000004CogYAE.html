Background
KrisFlyer will be expanding our partnership with Marriott Bonvoy, one of the world’s largest hotel loyalty programmes.

To be eligible to enrol and participate in the Preferred Partnership Program, a person,
 
  • must be a Joint Member
  • have a valid Marriott Bonvoy account
  • have a valid KrisFlyer account

Joint Members must link their Marriott Bonvoy Account and KrisFlyer Account by enrolling in the Preferred Partnership Program to participate in, and receive the benefits of, the Preferred Partnership Program.

Updates
Presently, conversions from Marriott Bonvoy points to KrisFlyer miles and from KrisFlyer miles to Marriott Bonvoy points are already enabled. 

From 13 December 2023, members of KrisFlyer Elite Silver status and above are eligible for Marriott Bonvoy status match and/or accelerator programme to be awarded a higher Marriott Bonvoy status, and vice versa.



Tier Upgrade
A registered member is eligible for an accelerated tier upgrade once they have fulfilled a certain number of flights.
KF to KF Elite Silver2 flights
KF Elite Silver to KF Elite Gold4 flights

If a member enquired on how long it takes for the tier upgrade to be reflected, refer below script.
 “You will receive the upgraded status within 4 to 6 weeks upon completion of the latest qualifying flight”
