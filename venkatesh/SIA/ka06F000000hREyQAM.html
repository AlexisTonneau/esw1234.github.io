Q. How will I know if I can be confirmed on my waitlisted flights? 

A. We will send an email/SMS or call notification on the contact details updated on your booking once the waitlisted flight is confirmed.

 

 

Q. I am a Krisflyer member and have put myself on waitlist on a few flights to Sydney next week. How is my chance in getting a confirmation on any of the flights? 

A. Waitlist confirmation is still subject to seat availability. You will be notified via email/SMS or call based on the contact information appended on your booking once your waitlisted flight is confirmed. As an option, you may check for available flights on a higher cabin or search for an alternative dates and flights where seats are available.

 

 

Q. My boss is a Solitaire PPS member who is currently waitlisted on Business class. He needs to travel to London for a business meeting in 2 days’ time. Would you be able to confirm an economy class seat for him?

A. Yes. We will confirm an economy class seat on the waitlisted flight. You may wish to know that as a PPS member, he will have a guaranteed seat on Economy class on Singapore Airlines. The request must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled time of departure even if you’ve booked through a travel agent or another airline.
