For further assistance, please contact your local Singapore Airlines office. (Error code: N0046)		Duplicate enrolment; member is already an existing KrisFlyer member.

For minor enrolment, member is using a non-unique email for his minor/child.

Pax is already successfully enrolled to KF, pending authentication (within 24hr)
1. Verify in MARMS/CEM member's enrolment details entered (email, first name, last name and DOB); likely due to member being an existing KF member.
 
2. Please use MARMS to check through the two validation checks below:
a. First validation check:
Email address (Enrolment will fail if duplicate email is used)

b. Second validation check:
(i) Last name, first character of First name and DOB
(ii) Last name and DOB (Enrolment will fail if there is an existing match of first name, last name and DOB)

Note that MARMS is unable to detect spacing between names so Tan/ZhiRong and Tan/Zhi Rong will register as separate names hence the request to check for first character of First name (Z in the example). Combination with date of birth will narrow down the list of members with similar name.
 
3. If an account is found with same name and birthdate, and pax deny it belongs to them, further verify using these fields: 
  • Passport number, passport expiry date 
  • Nationality
  • Mailing address
  • Mobile details
  • Mileage Accruals
  • Pax Manifest
Proceed with offline enrolment if pax denies existing account (could be genuine same name/DOB)


4. If member is attempting enrolment for his child, please advise member that unique email address for child needs to be used.
 
5. If caller has existing KF account and has forgotten password, guide pax to reset password details on SAA.

6. If email is used by another KF member in MARMS and email address belongs to caller, activate stop indicator for the email in the KF member and advise customer (owner of email address) to use a different email address for the new KF account. Explain to customer that SIA has reached out to the other KF to correct their email. Until then, regrettably, the customer is not able to use the email to sign up for KF account.

7. If caller have previously tried to enrol (in the last 24 hours) and did not click on the verification link in the email, advise caller to enrol again after 24 hours as the account that is pending authentication will be purged.

8. If the enrolment was done within 24hrs, and member claim did not receive pending authentication email, agent should re-confirm the email address of the member and re-trigger the pending authentication email via (MARMS > Customer Servicing > Customer Management > Account Authentication).

Note:
  • Escalate to DM/CCM if customer refuse to use a different email address.
  • Ensure to resolve the issue till pax is able to complete enrolment online.
  • Do not reveal details of existing KF whose record shows the email to the customer.
