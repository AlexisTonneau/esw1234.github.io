There are 3 types of Child meals: CHML, CHMLA, CHMLV.

ServiceDescription
Cut-off time for Child meal requestAt least 24hours prior to flight departure.

*(Please check Smart Tool-Inflight Meals as there are stations with exceptions)
ApplicationAdditional text is allowed. Restricted to medical or religious only. Booking status in ARD – “HN”
Passenger & segment relateYes
Eligibility/Notes for
Child Meal Western (CHML) default
Child Meal Asian (CHMLA)
Child Meal Vegetarian (CHMLV)		Child passenger with ticketed seats (above 2 years of age)

There is no MOML child meal.

Pax can update on “Manage Booking” page to an alternative child meal type. Once selected, the same cuisine type will apply for all meals for the ticketed journey.

CHML can be offered to a non-commercial PNR (NCP) child passenger. Refer here.

All children with a ticketed seat are eligible for Adult special meals/inflight meals. Agents can offer them to passenger if CHML/CHMLV/CHMLA is not suitable or unavailable.

As CHML/CHMLV/CHMLA is available only for children between age of 2 and below 12 years old, approval will need to be sought from IS&D if an adult passenger would like to request for one. Note that such requests are only acceded to in extenuating circumstances.
Book the Cook – Yummy!Children travelling in Suites, First or Business Class are eligible to request meal from the Book the Cook – Yummy! Menu.

Passengers can request in SAA under Manage Booking page.

To be requested at least 24 hours before flight departure.

Due to operational reasons, Pre-ordering of Yummy meals will not be available.


Refer to SAA for more details.

*For the breakdown of meals offered from stations, refer to Smart Tool - Inflight Meals.
