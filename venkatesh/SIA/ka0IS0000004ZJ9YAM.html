1.Special Meal (SPML) - General Policy


a. SPML refers to both standard SQ In-house (5 letter coded) and IATA (4 letter coded) meal options. Refer to SAA for detailed info on the SPML we offer.

b. SPML requests made at least 24 hours before flight departure can be acceded by contact centre agents if the meal response is reflected as HK.


c. There are 2 SPMLs with exceptions – KSML and NFMLA that require to be selected at least 48 hours before flight departure.

d. Some stations may require longer lead time due to double-catering or depending on their caterer’s ability to prefer and uplift the meal. Note the following stations requires more time

      • At least 48 hours prior to flight departure – Istanbul.
      • At least 32 hours prior to flight departure – Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Bangkok, Brunei, Cebu, Da Nang, Darwin, Davao, Denpasar, Dhaka, Hanoi, Hyderabad, Jakarta, Kathmandu, Kochi, Male, Medan, Penang, Phnom Penh, Phuket, Siem Reap, Surabaya and Yangon


e. Passengers cannot request two different SPMLs on the same flight.

f. Refer to Smart Tool on 1Point for the list of meals that stations can cater. Smart Tool will be updated to reflect the SPML available in each station.

3. FAQ

Q. Can I request for the same BTC for both meal service served on board the flight I am taking?
A. Only 1 BTC can be ordered per meal service if BTC service is available. i.e. BTC Beef Steak is not offered for Breakfast; thus, it cannot be ordered for a Breakfast Meal service.
 
Q. Can I request a Muslim Meal with no eggs on my flight from London in Premium Economy Class?
A. We are sorry as we have ceased all non-standard SPML requests for passengers travelling in Premium Economy and Economy Class.
 

Q. I have just changed my flight to travel tomorrow morning. As the flight I am taking is within 24hours, will I still be able to request a seafood meal on the flight?
A. We are sorry, but all meal requests must be made at least 24 hours before your flight departs.
 

Q. My mother’s birthday falls a week before her flight. Can you put in a request for a birthday cake for her?
A. We are sorry that we are unable to accommodate the request as the departure date does not fall on your mother's birthday. If asked, inform passenger BCAKE policy is +/-1 or 3 days (based on cabin class) from birthday. No other information should be shared.

Exceptional Handling: (handling guide for staff only, only highlighted policies can be shared)
+/- 1 day from the departure date – for everyone in SCL and YCL
+/- 3 days from the departure date – for FCL and JCL
+/- 3 days from the departure date – for PPS
+/- 7 days from the departure date – for HV

Q. Is the Muslim meal catered onboard SQ flights Halal?
A. The Muslim meal option offered to our passengers on board is prepared without the use of pork, lard or alcohol.
